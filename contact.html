Contact

Last updated: May 8, 2026

For questions about calcnote.io, policy requests, or technical issues, use the channels below.

Support and General Questions

Email: support@calcnote.io

Typical topics: bug reports, UI behavior, language issues, accessibility concerns, and feature requests.

Privacy and Data Questions

Email: privacy@calcnote.io

If your request concerns local storage behavior, cookies, or advertising-related privacy choices, include your browser name, version, and device type so issues can be reproduced more quickly.

What to Include in a Bug Report

  • Exact steps to reproduce the problem
  • Expected behavior and actual behavior
  • Browser and OS version
  • A screenshot or short screen recording when possible

Response Times

Support requests are reviewed in batch. Response time can vary based on volume and issue complexity. Critical breakages that prevent core use of the editor are prioritized.
