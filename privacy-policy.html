This Privacy Policy explains how calcnote.io collects, uses, stores, and discloses information when you use the website.

calcnote.io is a browser-based calculator notebook that lets you write formulas and notes directly in the page. At the moment, the service is designed to work primarily on the client side inside your browser.

2. Information Stored in Your Browser

calcnote.io stores some information locally in your browser using local storage so the app can remember your preferences and content between visits.
